Be Admitted To Shandong University

Shandong University (SDU), founded in 1901, is one of China's oldest and most prestigious universities. It is located in Shandong Province, with its main campus in Jinan and additional campuses in Qingdao and Weihai. Over more than a century, SDU has grown into a comprehensive and research-oriented institution, offering a wide range of disciplines, including science, engineering, medicine, humanities, social sciences, law, and business.

Got Silver Medal in ICPC 2025 Xi'an

The International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) is one of the most prestigious competitive programming competitions in the world. It brings together university students from across the globe to solve challenging algorithmic and computational problems under strict time constraints. Founded in 1970, ICPC has grown into a multi-tiered competition, including regional contests that lead to the world finals, which attract top teams from over 100 countries.
